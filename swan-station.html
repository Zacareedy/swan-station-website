DHARMA INITIATIVE — STATION 3 OF 6 — "THE SWAN"

DOC: DI-SWN-MAN-001  ·  REV: 14  ·  CLASSIFICATION: INTERNAL USE ONLY

The SWAN station was established in 1980 following the INCIDENT of April 23rd. The station's primary function is the controlled release of electromagnetic energy buildup through a designated operator input sequence administered via this terminal every 108 minutes.

PROTOCOL 23: Every 108 minutes, the designated operator must input the authorised numeric sequence into the Input Terminal. Failure to execute within the window will result in uncontrolled electromagnetic discharge.

AUTHORISED SEQUENCE — EYES ONLY — LEVEL 4:
The sequence consists of six two-digit values. They are to be memorised and never recorded in written form. Consult the orientation materials or your station chief if the sequence has been lost.
HINT: The terminal can help. The sequence is derivable. Begin with HELP.

The origin of the sequence is documented in the Valenzetti Equation (ref: Special Projects file VK-108). The numbers represent core environmental and human factors whose values, if unchanged, lead to a specific outcome. Their meaning beyond this station's function is not the concern of station personnel.

NOTE FROM V. KELVIN, SR. TECH OFFICER (CYCLE 9341): Do not attempt to determine what happens if the sequence is not entered. Trust the protocol. Do not leave the station. Do not go outside. Do not look at the sky on a clear night.
